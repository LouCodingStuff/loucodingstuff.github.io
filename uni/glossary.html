Glossary

Welcome to the glossary page. This page is dedicated to giving some defintions to words that are used across this website (and other defintions) in which the meanings might not be immediately clear too.

Transgender

Transgender is the word given to a person whose gender identity does not align with the sex registered for them at birth.

Cisgender

Cisgender is the word given to a person whose gender identity corresponds with the sex registered for them at birth, i.e. not transgender

Women/Woman

On his site the words Women and Women are being used to describe someone who identifies as a Woman.

Men/Man

On his site the words Men and Man are being used to describe someone who identifies as a Man.

Male/Female

On his site the words Male and Female are being used to describe the body in which the sex was assigned at birth.
